Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Description

This is how you make the perfect scrambled eggs. Impress your friends and never leave them hungry

Directions

  1. Crack eggs into bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Heat butter in frying pan on low for 3-5 minutes
  4. Scramble eggs in bowl
  5. Add eggs to frying pan
  6. Scrape pan and flip eggs so that the wet egs are heated
  7. Once the eggs are completely cooked remove from pan
  8. Serve and enjoy!
